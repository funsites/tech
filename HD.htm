How to Rename your Hard Drive or USB Flash Drive

Last edited 3rd January 2016


Hello there, this tutorial is going to show you how to rename your hard drive or USB Flash Drive


Step: 1 First, you need to find or plug in your device.

Step: 2 Now, right click it.

Step: 3 You will see a rename button near the bottom of your menu.

Step: 4 Now, you can rename it whatever you want, so that's how to rename your hard drive or your pen drive!
