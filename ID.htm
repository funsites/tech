How to enable icloud drive on your apple device

Last edited: 2nd January 2016

Hi today im going to show you how to enable icloud drive on your apple device


Step 1: First you go into settings

Step 2: Then you go into Icloud

Step 3: And go into icloud drive

Step 4: Click on the button beside icloud drive

Step 5: Then your icloud drive will appear on your home screen, so thats how you enable icloud drive Enjoy!
