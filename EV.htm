How to edit a video on Microsoft Windows

Last edited: 4th January 2016


Want to make a movie? Here's how:


Step 1: Gather or record footage you want for the movie.

Step 2: Open Windows Movie Maker.

Step 3: Drag the clips in and order them how you want.

Step 4: Press the File menu, hover over Save movie and press For computer.

Step 5: Done!
