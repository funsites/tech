How to Add Another Email on your default Email App on your Apple Device

Last edited 3rd January 2016


Hello, this tutorial will show you how to add a new email to the Apple Mail app.


Step 1: You First need to go into your "settings"

Step 2: Then go into "Mail, Contacts, Calendars" And press Add Account

Step 3: And there is different options Like "iCloud, Exchange, Google, YAHOO!, AOL. And Outlook.com so Thats how you add another account so hope there is definitely an option for your type of email, Enjoy!
