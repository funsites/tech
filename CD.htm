Hello im going to show you how to burn a CD


Step 1: Put a CD-R into Disc Drive

Step 2: Then open your PC Then Select The CD-R

Step 3: Select The songs That you want to burn on your Disc, and drag them in to your disc folder

Step 4: Then Click the button Burn To Disc at the top of your menu

Step 5: Your disc is ready to play, Enjoy Your Music
