Burn a CD

Last edited: 3rd January 2016

Hello, this tutorial is going to show you how to burn a CD


Step 1: Put a CD-R disc into your Disc Drive

Step 2: Then open "My Computer" and select The CD-R icon

Step 3: Select the songs that you want to burn on to your disc, and drag them in to your disc's folder

Step 4: Then click the button "Burn To Disc" at the top of your menu

Step 5: A loading screen will appear, and when it's finished loading it will say "Done" and your disc will be ready to play! Enjoy your music!
